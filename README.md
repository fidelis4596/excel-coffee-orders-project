The goal of this project is to analyse coffee sales data to understand customer purchasing patterns, top-selling coffee types, sales trends over time, and country-level performance. ## ‚ùì Key Questions (KPIs)
- How do total sales trends change over time for each coffee type?
- Which country contributes the most to overall sales?
- Which coffee types and roast levels generate the highest sales?
- Do loyalty card holders contribute more to total sales than non-holders?
- Who are the Top 5 customers by total purchase value?


## ‚öôÔ∏è Process
1. Checked the dataset for missing values, duplicates, and inconsistencies.
2. Cleaned and standardized the data types (dates, text fields, currencies, sizes).

4. Built PivotTables to answer each KPI.

## üîç Project Insights
- **United States** is the highest-contributing country, generating the most revenue.
- **Light Roast** appears as one of the most popular selections when filters are applied.
- **Top customer spent approximately $317**, making them an important high-value client.
- Loyalty card holders contribute a significant portion of total sales, indicating strong customer retention value.
- Coffee types such as **Arabica and Excelsa** show strong and consistent sales peaks over time.
  
##  Final Conclusion
The analysis indicates that the business should focus on its strongest markets and customer segments‚Äîparticularly customers from the United States, who generate the highest sales. High-performing coffee types such as Arabica and Excelsa, along with popular roast levels like Light Roast, should be prioritized in marketing and inventory planning.
To drive further growth, the business can strengthen customer loyalty, target high-value buyers, and use seasonal trends to boost sales during peak months.

### Recommended strategic actions:
- Personalized product recommendations based on coffee type preferences.
- Enhanced loyalty rewards to retain high-value customers.
- Seasonal promotions aligned with historical sales peaks.
- Country-specific marketing campaigns, especially for the United States market.

These strategies will help increase sales, improve customer retention, and support long-term business growth.
